Frequently Asked Questions

  • 01 What services does Rogahrt provide?

    A Digital dashboard consisting of E-prescription,Patient Case History Database, Analytics Matrix and a disease database

  • 02 Validity of E-prescriptions?

    As per government norms(acts to be mentioned) any medical practitioner can provide E-prescription with regsitered license number certified by MCI. This License number will be present in every E-prescription form generated by Rogahrt.

  • 03 Who can register?

    All the doctors with valid Medical License Number,who fall under MBBS and its respective specializations, BDS,DDS and MDS, BAMS and, BVSc and MVSc. are eligible to enroll.
